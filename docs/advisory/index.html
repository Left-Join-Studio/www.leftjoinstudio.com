Structure of the One-Pager

1. Headline

  • Transform Your Business with Premium Tech Advisory Services

2. Subheadline

  • Unlock unparalleled value and accelerate your success with expert guidance from a seasoned tech advisor.

3. Introduction

  • About Me: A brief, engaging introduction that highlights your extensive experience and accomplishments in the tech industry. Mention your 20+ years of experience, your journey from a 10x developer to a strategic advisor, and your track record of delivering real value to clients.

4. The Offer

  • Exclusive Advisory Services: Limited to 5 clients per month, ensuring personalized and dedicated attention.
  • 5 Hours Per Week: Flexible scheduling to meet your needs, whether it’s strategy sessions, problem-solving, or hands-on guidance.

5. Unique Value Proposition

  • Why Choose Me?: Highlight your differentiators.
    • Proven Expertise: Over two decades of experience in the tech industry, including leading successful projects and startups.
    • Strategic Insight: Ability to identify the real problems and deliver solutions that create true value.
    • Efficiency: Delivering answers and results in a fraction of the time compared to others, saving you time and money.
    • Tailored Solutions: Customized strategies and advice based on your unique needs and goals.

6. Key Benefits

  • Rapid Results: Achieve your objectives faster with expert guidance.
  • Cost-Effective: While the service is premium-priced, the efficiency and effectiveness of the solutions provided result in significant cost savings and ROI.
  • Focused Attention: Limited client roster ensures you receive the focus and dedication you deserve.
  • Scalable Solutions: Strategies that not only solve immediate problems but also set you up for long-term success.

7. Client Success Stories

  • Testimonials: Include brief, powerful testimonials from past clients that highlight the impact of your advisory services.
  • Case Studies: Summarize a couple of case studies that demonstrate your ability to deliver value quickly and effectively.

8. Call to Action

  • Ready to Transform Your Business?: Encourage potential clients to take the next step.
  • Schedule a Free Consultation: Provide a link to schedule a free initial consultation to discuss their needs and how you can help.

9. Contact Information

  • Reach Out Today: Include your contact details and a link to your website or a specific landing page for the advisory service.

Differentiator

Your differentiator is your unique combination of extensive experience, strategic insight, and efficiency. You’re not just another advisor; you’re someone who has been in the trenches, understands the intricacies of the tech industry, and can deliver actionable insights and solutions quickly. Highlight your ability to cut through the noise and focus on what truly matters, providing value in a tenth of the time others might take.

Promoting the Premium Pricing

  • Emphasize ROI: Clearly communicate the return on investment clients can expect. While the upfront cost is higher, the efficiency and effectiveness of your solutions lead to greater long-term savings and success.
  • Highlight Efficiency: Stress that your ability to deliver results quickly means less time spent on trial and error, fewer resources wasted, and faster achievement of goals.
  • Showcase Success Stories: Use testimonials and case studies to demonstrate how your premium service has led to significant, measurable outcomes for clients.
  • Exclusive Access: The limited number of clients ensures that each one receives your undivided attention and bespoke solutions, which justifies the premium pricing.
